What is Red Light Therapy?

Red Light Therapy (RLT), also known as photobiomodulation, uses specific wavelengths of red and near-infrared light to stimulate cellular function. Unlike UV light, red light is completely safe and non-invasive — it works at the cellular level to promote healing and regeneration.

Our Trifecta 450 Pro system uses 450 medical-grade LED panels to deliver therapeutic wavelengths of light deep into your body's tissues. This FDA-approved technology has been shown to support a wide range of health and wellness goals.
