About Angela

Angela O. Roush is a highly respected member of the American Massage Therapy Association (AMTA) and the proud owner of Serenity Therapeutic Massage Spa, LLC — located inside The Joni Salon House in Liberty, North Carolina.

With expertise across a wide range of therapeutic modalities, Angela offers a unique retreat that promises an exceptional massage experience. Her extensive expertise and unwavering commitment to perfection ensure that every client receives the utmost care and relaxation during their visit.

Angela is also certified in Cranial Sacral Therapy by the International Association of Healthcare Practitioners, providing solace and relief to those suffering from intense pain. She is a certified Lymphatic Drainage Therapist specializing in orthopedic, liposuction, and post-mastectomy recovery.

At Serenity Therapeutic Massage, Angela invites you to discover a haven where serenity and healing beautifully come together. With 431 verified reviews and a 4.9-star rating, your journey to wellness is in expert hands.
