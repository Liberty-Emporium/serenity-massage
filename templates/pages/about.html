About Angela

Angela Roush is a dedicated and passionate licensed massage therapist based in Liberty, North Carolina. With years of experience in therapeutic massage and holistic wellness, Angela founded Serenity Therapeutic Massage Wellness to provide her community with professional, personalized care.

Specializing in a variety of massage techniques including Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone therapy, and cutting-edge red light therapy with the Trifecta 450 Pro, Angela tailors each session to meet her clients' unique needs.

Angela believes in the power of touch therapy to heal, restore, and rejuvenate. Her warm, welcoming approach ensures every client feels comfortable and cared for from the moment they walk through the door.
